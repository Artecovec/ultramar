About Company

Ultramar was established in 2007 as logistic operator with offices in two largest ports of the Baltic Sea — Riga and Saint-Petersburg. In 2009 the first Ultramar terminal on transshipment of mineral fertilizers from wagons to containers without intermediate storage (wagon/vessel) was constructed in Saint-Petersburg.

In 2010 the new directions development and transportation volume increase caused construction of the second phase of the transshipment terminal. So far, Ultramar is the largest transporter of mineral fertilizers in containers. In 2011 the company transported over 1 mln. tons of fertilizers from the Baltic Sea ports to ports throughout the world.

Ultramar is proud of having as partners the largest Russian manufacturers of mineral fertilizers:

  1. Acron.
  2. PhosAGRO.
  3. Uralchem.
  4. Minudobreniya.

Ultramar heavily invests in development and improvement of technologies which allow providing even more high-quality and rapid transshipments and deliveries of partners’ cargo.

The main Ultramar objectives:

  1. High quality service.
  2. Reliability.
  3. Predictability of relations with partners.
  4. Operational efficiency.

Services

Ultramar provides whole range of services on transportation of mineral fertilizers in containers from the Baltic Sea ports to the whole world.

Company profile

  • delivery of mineral fertilizers from the factory to port in wagons;
  • transshipment from wagons to containers;
  • delivery of containers to any part of the world.

To implement these objectives Ultramar has own:

  • carrying railway vehicle;
  • specialized transshipment terminal;
  • service contracts with the largest container lines.

From wagons to containers

Transshipment of mineral fertilizers from wagons to containers is made directly, without intermediate storage (wagon — container). Maximum capacity of the terminal — 72 wagons per day. Terminal accepts and forwards Hopper wagons by route dispatch. The largest container dispatch from the Ultramar terminal to the container terminals of Saint-Petersburg is made by blocktrains.

Packing of mineral fertilizers

Packing is made directly from wagons to bags or big-bags, further loaded to containers of the following standard sizes:

  • 20- and 40-feet standard container;
  • 20- and 40-feet standard refrigerator container.

Total capacity of packing equipment — 1000 tons per day.

Possible packaging volumes:

  • bags — 25 kg, 50 kg;
  • big-bags — 500 kg, 1000 kg, 1250 kg.

Transshipment of liquid mineral fertilizers

Transshipment is made directly from tanks to containers equipped with flexi-tanks. Total transshipment capacity — 1500 tons per day.

General information about the terminal

Terminal capacity — 8000 TEU. Throughput — 5 trains per day (300 wagons). The terminal also used for transshipment of packed cargo from the covered wagons and open-top wagons to containers.
